The 1 ♣ opening

Opening 1 ♣ shows at least 16+ hcp, and is forcing. The continuation is fairly natural.

Some hands might be upgraded to 1 ♣ due to distribution, but wildly distributional hands might also be downgraded to avoid problems if the opponents preempt.

The 1 ♦ negative

Responding 1 ♦ shows a hand which doesn't have enough values to establish a game force.
